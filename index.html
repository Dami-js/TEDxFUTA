Akinyemi Jude

2021 TEDxFUTA Lincensee

Jude Akinyemi is a smart, goal-oriented, and hardworking individual with vast knowledge in leadership and development.

The Oyo-born genius has served in various capacities, ranging from student leadership to community service and capacity building. He is committed to equipping minds, enlightening intellectuals with ideas on how to make a difference and he's a firm believer in community engagement as a tool for global development.

Jude has volunteered for different various organizations - both local and international - in various capacities. Amongst which includes: JCIN, FUTA, Hands-On Deck Community, and YALI Network, Ibadan.

He hopes to give back to the society through the TEDxFUTA just as he has done with other notable mentions above. He is the Lincensee for the year's event, after volunteering in 2019.
